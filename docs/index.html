About

A former employee enters the office building of Catch 2022. After decades of loyal work he was fired, as he was wrongfully accused of and blamed for stealing a co-worker's lunch. The CEO at the top floor was part of the decision, as well as the decision to make the elevators only move one floor at a time. Something has happened to the office objects though...

Fast-paced Action

Experience combat with a high tempo and utilize your shoot, dash, and parry abilities to survive through hordes of enemies. Proceed the levels with caution or your demise will be quick.

Time-based Score

To complete a level, you must kill all the enemies and then find the exit. For every run you will be scored based on how fast you complete the levels. Get faster completion to beat your own and your friends' scores!

Powerups

Powerups are placed throughout the levels. Gather them and get buffs that help you wreak havoc on your enemies.
